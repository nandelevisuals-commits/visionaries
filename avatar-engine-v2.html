Higgsfield Soul ID — Best Practices
Input Images

Use 5–15 photos minimum. All must show the same hairstyle — mixed styles will cause Soul ID to average between them and lose consistency.

Image Variety

Include multiple angles (front, 3/4, side), different lighting conditions, and different expressions. No sunglasses. Face must be clearly visible.

Resolution & Quality

Use the highest quality images available. Avoid blurry, compressed, or heavily filtered photos. Natural lighting performs better than flash.

Prompt Anchoring

Always end prompts with: "Ensure facial consistency is identical to the reference image with hyper realistic skin texture and open pores, exact facial hair and symmetry as the reference."

Character Sheet First

Generate your Nanobanana 2 character sheet before creating your Soul ID. Upload the character sheet as one of your Soul ID reference inputs for stronger consistency locks.

Nanobanana 2 Tips

Upload both your character sheet AND a product/scene reference image together. Include "Ensure product consistency" and "facial consistency" anchors in every prompt.
